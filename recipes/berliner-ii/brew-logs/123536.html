08/17/2014 6:57am 24.0 degC —

[1 day] Added about 150mL thick slurry of Brett (Wyeast 5226 - 3rd gen), applied heat belt to maintain 24°C.

08/20/2014 8:47am 13.0 degC 1.004

[4 days] Removed heat belt after 3 days - dropped down a lot in temp, but seems to be fairly well finished anyway. Checked pH and got 3.35 this time (might need to recalibrate, or the Brett might have raised it). Quite tart, with very clean lactic sourness, sweetish finish - a little wheaty. Only the slightest hint of Brett funk - guessing that will take a while to develop. Reckon it might be good to bottle in a week!
08/16/2014 8:23am 39.0 degC 1.032

09/19/2014 3:15am 15.0 degC 1.006

[34 days] Rhubarb version bottled after 16 days with the stems. Took on an excellent pink colour and some rhubarb flavour. FG rose to 1.006 for some reason, possibly because something leeched out of the rhubarb into solution - Brett will ultimately deal with that though. Filled 5 x 750mL bottles, giving each 2 carbonation drops.

08/16/2014 5:27am

Mashed in with 7L water and hit 59°C; heat on HIGH, stirring constantly.
[10min] Mash at 66.3°C; heat OFF.
[26min] Mash at 65.7°C; heat still OFF.
[45min] Took 3L decoction and brought to boil.
[55min] Decoction at boiling point; boiled for 10mins.
[65min] Returned decoction to main mash and hit 75°C. Held for 5mins.
[70min] Poured entire mash into grain bag and let drain over 10mins.
[80min] Put grain bag into 10L additional water at 77°C, stirred.
[90min] Collected 15L wort at 1.035 SG.
Raised temp of wort to ~80-85°C for 10-15mins to pasteurise, then cooled to 40°C before pitching Lacto starter.
