Notes

Date Note
12/07/2014 8:03pm

Plan to do a BIAB w/ roughly 4 gallons of water (507 ounces from 5x 3L Arrowhead spring water = 3.96 gallons), then add additional sugars and top up to meet gravity / volume.

Started heating strike. Began to grind grains. Go through several pounds and the Barley Crusher seized up. Troubleshot; cleaned out and opened up gap a little bit.

Completed first and second grind and set aside to store. Brought water to boil for a few moments, covered and wrapped in plastic wrap.
12/09/2014 2:00am

Fired up kettle. Brought strike up to 166F. Added 1/2 tbsp. 5^2 ph Stabilizer. Added grain bag and stirred. Temp settled at 159F. Covered for mash rest.
12/09/2014 3:35am

After lauter and sparge ended up with 4.16 gallons at 1.061. Wanted to top-up to a 5 gallonish batch. Used http://www.brewersfriend.com/dilution-and-boiloff-gravity-calculator/ and predicted:
Adding 1.58 gallons = 5.74 gallons at 1.044
Adding 2.38 gallons = 6.54 gallons at 1.039

Decided volume > gravity, added the 3 remaining 101.4 fl oz. containers of water. Measured 2" from top of kettle = 6.66 gallons of wort (that's pleasant). Measured gravity at 10% brix, 1.040 SG.

12/09/2014 4:28am

GS' recipe looked about 20ish IBU. Calculated 1.4 oz 5.5% AA EKG at 60 and 1 oz at 5 mins to get 25 IBU with 1.040 wort.

Began collecting spice additions. GS' recipe was rather aggressive, but I'll trust it.
- 2x 5" cinnamon sticks (likely cassia, was the cheap stuff)
- 1x nutmeg (GS called for 1 "seed". Seems like a lot. Cracked a whole nutmeg and threw it in)
- 1/2x vanilla bean (thought I had 1 whole. Just used the half I had left)
- 7x allspice berries
- 8x coriander seeds
- 1.5 tsp whole cloves (seems like a lot here too...)
- 1x navel orange peel (Fresh. Couldn't find a nectarine, winged it. Tried to avoid pith.)

Collected sugars:
- 1/2 can (227 g) of Lyle's golden syrup
- 2 oz Breer Rabbit molasses
- 1.5 lbs wild desert honey
12/09/2014 6:18am

60 min addition of 1.4 oz EKG
15 min addition of sugars.
10 min addition of 1 whirfloc.
5 min addition of 1 oz EKG

At flameout, put in immersion chiller, paddle, probe and the muslin package of spices. Chilled, stirring. At 10 mins pulled spices. Continued until

12/17/2014 5:21am

In the morning, dropped fridge down under 50F. Pulled carboy in the evening. Cleaned keg. Racked into keg and put 16 psi for carbonation. Measured 8% brix, corrected to 1.012 FG.
