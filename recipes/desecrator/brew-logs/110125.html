Notes

Date Note
01/17/2014 4:18am

Created yeast starter. Used 65g Breiss Golden Light DME boiled for 5 mins with 650 ml of bottled spring water. Cooled. Added 1/8th tsp of WLN1000-HB yeast nutrient and put on stir plate.
01/19/2014 3:40pm

Boiled 1 gallon water. Ground grains. Began heating strike water.
01/19/2014 4:27pm

Doughed-in with 134F strike. Mash temp leveled at 126F. Pulled 15ish 1qt (fullish) spoons of thick mash after a few minutes. Main mash temp kept at around 126F.

01/19/2014 4:56pm

Boiled first decoction for 3 minutes. Pulled roughly 6 qts of thick mash from the decoction to reach 147F on the main mash. Main mash pH low - between 4.6 and 5.0. 12.6% brix measured.
01/19/2014 5:10pm

Returned remaining decoction back to mash tun with 2 min remaining in 20 min beta sacch rest. Main mash at 143F.
01/19/2014 5:24pm

Pulled 9 qts of thick mash and boiled. Boiled 3 mins.

01/19/2014 5:35pm

Returned entire amount to tun. 151F in mash tun. Soaked and added rice hulls after soaking in hot water. Began boiling 1 qt water. Measured 17.4% brix, 5.0 - 5.4 pH. At 7 mins, added 1 qt boiling. Came up to 152ishF.

01/19/2014 6:03pm

Pulled 2 gallons of thin mash (1 1/2 from hose, sparge stuck, pulled 1/2 with a wee bit of thick from the top). Brought to boil. While waiting for boil measured 5.0ish pH, 18.2% brix.

01/19/2014 6:19pm

Added boiled thin mash to main mash. Temp up to 162F for mash out (well, almost mash out).
01/19/2014 6:38pm

Tried to lauter - FAILED. Pulled less than 1 gallon and stuck. Transferred entire mash to secondary containment. Rinsed out PVC screens. Soaked another 8 oz of rice hulls.
01/19/2014 7:00pm

Added 1 gallon wort to tun. Added rice hulls. Added mash back to tun and lautered. Mash cloudy. Still 18.5% brix, pH is 5 or lower. Iodine test has been purple throughout; seems to be less-so now. Heated sparge to 175. Ran 6 7/8" of wort (1058.75 in3, 4.58 gallons). Added enough 175F water to reach top of grist level. Let sit 15 mins.
01/19/2014 7:44pm

Drew first runnings. Measured (below). Added remainder of 5 gallons of sparge water. Drew second runnings. 10.6% brix, 5.0pH.

01/19/2014 9:32pm

Brought to a boil and added 2 oz Hallertau. After 30 mins measured gravity at 16.5% / 1.068. Decided to boil 90 mins. After another 30 mins (at 30 mins) measured 17%. Added whifloc and 1/2 tsp of WLN1000-HB at 10 mins.
01/19/2014 11:02pm

Cooled to 82F. Measured 16.5% brix. Measured 3" from top of pot = 1540 in^3 = 6.66 gallons. Aerated for 5 mins with immersion blender. Transferred to 6 gallon carboy. Pitched Bavarian yeast. Had extra in pot - transferred to 32 oz growler and pitched 1 packet of Safale S-04.

01/27/2014 12:45am

Bottled 64 oz fermented w/ Safale S-04. Primed with 0.5 oz of dextrose "boiled" in the microwave with 8 oz water. Added roughly 2 oz to each of four 16 oz sanitized bottles. Poured from 64 oz growler into sanitized funnel to the bottles. Measured 7.5% brix / 1.006 FG.
01/28/2014 4:00pm

Bubbles in airlock down to 2 - 3 per minute. Increased temperature of thermostat from 48F to 58F for diacetyl rest.
02/02/2014 7:23pm

Racked to secondary for lagering. Measured 8.6% brix / 1.013 corrected SG. Reduced temp on thermostat to 56F
02/06/2014 2:51am

Feb 03, 2014: Reduced temp to 52F
Feb 04, 2014: Reduced temp to 48F
Feb 05, 2014: Reduced temp to 44F
02/07/2014 1:50am

Reduced temp to 40F
02/08/2014 4:00pm

Reduced temp to 36F.
03/09/2014 5:42pm

Boiled 2 cups of water with 4 oz of dextrose for 5 minutes. Cooled to 61F in ice bath. Added to sanitized bottling bucket. Measured beer at 8.4% brix / 1.012 FG. Racked 4 gallons of beer onto priming sugar mixture. Bottled 40 1/2 bottles. Tastes delicious. Slightly brownish in color, but crystal clear. Roasty nose, malty. Clean taste with heavy malt back bone. No diacetyl obvious at this time. Very pleased!
