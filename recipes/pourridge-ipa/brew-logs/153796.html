Brewed by Chris

On April 13, 2018

Brewing Notes

Brew day!

Start Time: 8:34 End Time:

Water PH: 5.5

Brewing Playlist: PUP, Camp Cope, Petal, mewithoutYOU,

Boiling a kettle of water to rinse and heat up mash cooler. - drained all water through spigot to help clear and gunk that might be in there.

Strike Water - 5 gallons of water at 170. Added half of a kettle of boiling water to the mash tun (part of my 5 gallons).

Started heating up 5 gallons of sparge water.

Mash temp at 9:30am is: 154F.

10:35 - started vorlauf. Took gravity of 1.065 and temp of wort is 125F. Should translate to around a first gravity reading of 1.077 10:47 - started collecting runnings. Collected 2.8 Gallons or wort from the first runnings.

11:18 - Added 5 gallons of 170-175F water. Also poured the first running gravity testing liquid back into the mash tun.

11:31 - started varlouf of batch sparge.

Gravity of second reading is 1.020 with a temp of 140. That translates to about 1.035

Added 2.5 gallons to the boil pot. already had 2.8 so should be at 5.3

added 2.7 gallons to make a total of 8 gallons of pre-boil wort.

It is insane outside (freezing rain, windy, just plain nasty) so I will attempt to boil on the stove.

Boil started at 12:30. Not insanely rolling boil but it is boiling!

1:20 - .25 of amarillo 1:27 - .25 of amarillo 1:30 - .25 of amarillo 1:35 - .25 of amarillo 1:45 - .5 of Simcoe, .5 of Cirta, .5 of Calypso, Irish Moss, 1:55 - 1.75 of Citra 2:00 flame off, boil done!

Gravity Reading is: 1.052 with a temp of 136F. Gravity was 1.067 (a pinch high but not bad)

Got 5.5 gallons (maybe slightly below?) from the boil kettle.

Used the power drill oxygenator to stir up the beer, poured out a little bit of the beer portion of the yeast starter and then pitched at 9am on April 16.

Added 1ox of Amarillo for the first addition of dry hops. Maybe added one day later than I wanted to, for next time add on Day 3 not day 4?
