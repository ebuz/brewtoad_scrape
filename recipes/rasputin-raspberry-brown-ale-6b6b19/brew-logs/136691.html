an All Grain Fruit Beer by Brewcat

Brewed by Brewcat

On August 02, 2015

Brewing Notes

Grainfather. Same recipe as Imperial Brown for barrel ageing, but reduced the scale to be more manageable- 6.5KG instead of 8.5.

Bottled 18L in mostly 330ml bottles on 5/9/15, straight from conical under CO2 pressure.

Taste

Taste Rating
45 / 50

25/10/15 Very good indeed. Lovely chocolatey background and intense raspberry flavours. Liked by everyone who has tasted it, even people who don't usually like darker beers.

Notes

Date Note
08/02/2015 11:45am

Started mash. Added 1tsp gypsum and 1/2 tsp MgSO4 to water.
08/02/2015 1:15pm

Mash-out at 76C for 15 min. 19.6 brix
08/02/2015 1:20pm

Sparged with 13L@76C while bringing to the boil.
08/02/2015 2:10pm

Started boil, 26.5L, 16.2 brix
08/02/2015 3:05pm

Down to 24L, so 2.5L/Hr evaporation. 17.9 brix
08/02/2015 3:45pm

Added late hops. 22.5L, 18.5 brix
08/02/2015 3:55pm

Attached cooling coil and recirculated to sanitise, then started cooling. 21.75L
08/02/2015 4:05pm

Down to 75C, started transferring at 21C to FV. 19.0 brix, hydrometer 1.082
08/02/2015 4:25pm

Finished transferring 19L to FV. Boiled 1L water and added back to boiler. Cooled and transferred to FV to take it to the target 20L. Mixed well, OG (hydrometer)= 1.078. 19.2 brix (1.079), so good correlation. Pitched 1 small jam jar of the yeast from the last batch. I did not clean out the FV from last time, as it is the same beer and I only emptied it a bit over a day ago, and it's been sealed, other than scrub off all the dried trub and yeast from the inside of the lid and spray with starsan.
08/02/2015 4:50pm

FV in position and hooked up to brewbelt set to 20C
08/17/2015 9:33pm

Removed some trub and yeast, raised to 22C and gave it a good shake to get yeast going again.
08/19/2015 9:33pm

Took off heatbelt, drained yeast, added 2KG raspberries, defrosted and heated to about 85C for 15 minutes.
08/26/2016 9:58pm

Almost a year to the day since brew day, poured a glass from a 330ml bottle. Highly carbonated, but little foam to gush, and head disappeared almost immediately. Nose of raspberries, some roastiness, some marmite. Taste, similar but also chocolate, really nice! Looks completely flat, but actually explodes in the mouth - lovely mouthfeel. Amazing how well the fruit is lasting. Long finish of fruit, roasted malts, and a slight tartness.
