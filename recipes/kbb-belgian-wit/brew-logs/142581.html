Brewing Notes

Spices: - 0.95 oz sweet orange peel - 0.5 corriander (whole, ground for use) - 2 cloves - 0.1 oz (1 tsp) juniper berries.
- 0.17637 oz (5 grams) chamomile

Hops: KBB Wit 16 5.83 Hallertau 1.50 2.70 4.05 1.062 60 0.95 0.22 20.71% 11

Notes

Date Note
04/23/2016 7:25pm

Ground:
- 6 lbs, 13 oz. GWM white wheat of varying ages (2014, 2015, 2016)
- 6 lbs Best Maltz pilsner

Added to malt bill:
- 1 lbs 1 oz flaked oats

Soaked 4 oz rice hulls in warm water.

Heated 4 gallons spring water, 0.5 gallons tap water in mash tun with direct infusion for strike.
04/23/2016 8:01pm

Heated strike water to 158 F. Doughed in rice hulls, then mixed malts. Stirred, cooled to 150 F per mash run thermometer. Checked opposite side of tun, closer to 158 F. Stirred, added heat. Cut heat, continued stirring. At 15 mins, 13.4% brix, 158 F.

04/23/2016 8:53pm

Mashed 60 mins. Ended about 154 F. Recirculated 2 gallons. 19% brix. 1.079 SG. Iodine test shows full extraction.
04/23/2016 9:11pm

Lautered. Gained 3 gallons. Added "some" (to top of grains) 195F sparge water. Stirred. 168F mash temp.
04/23/2016 9:31pm

Tried to sparge - stuck! Crap. Added remaining sparge water, stirred, let sit 15 minutes. Crossed fingers.

04/23/2016 9:51pm

Cleared stuck sparge w/ the ol' DD from a ThermaWorks probe. Measured runnings at 10.25% brix.
04/23/2016 11:57pm

Boiled 60 mins. Added 1.5 oz Hallertau at 60 mins. Added 1 tablet whirfloc at 10 mins. Added spice mixture and final 0.5 oz hops at 5 mins. Cooled to

04/24/2016 12:18am

Beer came out to 1.074 OG and only 4.5 gallons in the fermenter. Added 0.5 gallons of distilled water mixed with 1/2 tsp. of Wyeast yeast nutrient. Essentially topped up to 5 gallons, or 1.067 OG.

04/29/2016 2:00pm

Increased temp in ferm fridge to 65 F.
05/05/2016 3:37am

Set temp in fermentor back to 60 F.
05/19/2016 6:00am

Racked off of 4 1/2 - 4 3/4 gallons in fermenter to keg. Collected 4+ gallons. Measured 7.5% brix.
