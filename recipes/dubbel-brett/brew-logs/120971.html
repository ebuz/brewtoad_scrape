Fermentation Checks

Date Temperature Gravity Notes
07/14/2014 3:48am 12.0 degC 1.020

[14 days] Spent first 10 days with heat belt at 20-22°C, and last 4 days at ambient (very cool). Surprised at how well it's attenuated already. Taste is great - very much like a dubbel; malty, dark fruits, hint of roast, and the brett esters are pretty ideal for the style. Overall a bit musty/rough at the moment but will clean up as it ages no doubt.
08/17/2014 4:26am 13.0 degC —

[48 days] Racked to secondary with 1.5L reserved wort, then applied heat belt - hoping for a fairly healthy secondary fermentation before bottling in about 2 weeks. Still tasting excellent. Brett character is there, but at this age, it could easily be mistaken for Belgian phenolics.
08/31/2014 7:32am 20.0 degC 1.010

[62 days] Been in secondary for 2 weeks and has finished out - a little lower this time because of the temperature, I'd guess. Taste is really clean - possibly even cleaner than before, except now there's a hint of alcohol in the flavour too. Lots of spicy notes and dark malt character, with only a hint of leathery Brett character.

Today I added 10 oak cubes (Whiskey oak of unknown origin, which I'd boiled in water twice for a total of 1 hour to get rid of excess tannins). I think it will only need a week or so to pick up decent oak character.
08/12/2014 9:01am 13.0 degC 1.011

[43 days] This is expected FG, but could go a little more..? Tastes great - drier now, and nicely malty-sweet overall. Moderate Brett character, but very approachable (not weird-funky) and fits well with Dubbel style. Also very full bodied at the moment, which adds to my suspicion that it has some fermentation to go.
09/14/2014 4:31am 14.0 degC 1.009

[76 days] 2 weeks with oak has added only a very subtle woodiness, but a hint of whiskey character (nice!). Bottling some tomorrow with dextrose, then blending the remainder with my Flanders-style sour.
07/26/2014 5:50am 13.0 degC 1.015

[26 days] Still plenty of CO2 when roused. Hope to be able to apply heat belt to finish it off next week. Taste is still pretty clean, but it's a bit more funky/fruity than last time. Hides the 7.3% alcohol very well. Very solid flavour - malty and dry, hint of tartness.

06/30/2014 10:30am 20.0 degC 1.071

[0 days] Pitched entire yeast cake from Brett vs. Ella, with only minor aeration (splashing). Ambient is 17°C but will drop overnight.
