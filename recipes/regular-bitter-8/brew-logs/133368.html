Brewed by Nate

On March 29, 2015

Brewing Notes

First brew on new system. Ran it like a BrewEasy, RIMS the whole amount of water with half in the kettle and half in the tun pumping continuously.

Ran the kettle dry twice, fortunately the boilcoil survives OK. Had to take it apart after pumping to the tun to clean the element after some wort burned onto it.

Looks like I got about 70% efficiency which is fine. Next time the amount of water needs to go up a bit, maybe 9 gallons total to get 7.5 in the kettle.

Added 5g CaCl, 3g CaSO4, and 1g CaCO3. For 8gal this should give me 83ppm Ca, 91ppm Cl, 69ppm SO4, and 64ppm HCO3.

Taste

Possibly the best bitter I have made yet. Not too much caramel going on like the other versions did. There is a bit of a mineral bite to it in the finish that is probably due to the mash adjusting I did with the pH strips that didn't seem to do anything (I don't think they work, maybe too old?).
