Notes

Date Note
05/26/2014 1:17am

Mash in with 2.89 gal at 161.4

Put the grain in around 164 and the temp settled around 152. Added 1 cup of refrigerated water and stirred to bring the temp down. Got it down to around 150 and closed the lid on the cooler.

Checked at 30 minutes. Temps around the cooler were about 150. Didn't make any adjustments and closed the cooler for another 30 minutes

First runnings
1.4 gallons
1.062 @ 121 F
1.072 after correction
72*1.4=100.8 points

Added 2.9 gallons of sparge water to the drained grain at 188.3 F. Stirred the water into the grain hoping to get the temp to 168 F. Water temp leveled off around 157-161F. Need to bring the sparge water up to around 195 next time.

Sparge runnings
~2.9 gallons (Same as the amount added, dead weight was already full with previous water and grain.)
1.015 @ 142
1.031 after correction
31*2.9=89.9 points

Combined runnings
1.032 @ 133.3 F
1.045 after correction
45*4.25=191.25 (Measured)
100.8+89.9=190.7 (Estimated from adding the two sparges together)

Total wort volume after mash was 4.25 gallons.

Dead space
9.28 pounds of grain
9.28 * 0.1 = 0.928 gallons of water absorbed by grains
4.25 - 2.9 = 1.35 gallons from the first sparge
2.9 - 1.35 = 1.55 gallons lost to absorption and dead space
1.55 - 0.928 = 0.622 gallons mash tun dead space

Put exactly 4 gallons into the boil kettle. 180 points into the boil kettle (45*4=180)

100 minute boil. Followed the hop schedule after the first 40 minutes.

Removed from heat directly to ice bath. Two bags of ice and the ice in freezer cooled the wort to 73 F in ~15 minutes.

3 gallons to the fermenter at 1.061(Measured)
180/3=60 (1.060 estimated)

Pitched two packs of Safale US-05 and shook the fermentation bucket to aerate.

Put a blowoff tube on and put it into the freezer set on 65.

21 minutes from flame out to freezer closing.

Started fermentation on 5/4.

Safale US-05 has an 81% attenuation.
60 * .81 = 48.6 drop
60 - 48.6 = 11.4 ( 1.011 estimated FG)
48.6 / 7.5 = 6.48 % ABV (estimated)

Racked to secondary after 7 days
FG was at 1.011
~6.5% ABV before bourbon addition
Lost about 0.5 gallons to trub, 2.5 gallons to secondary
Racked on top of 3/4 cup bourbon and 1.5 vanilla beans
Racked to secondary on 5/11.

15 days in secondary, cold crashed the last three days at 37F. Began cold crash on 5/23.

Bottled with 1.75 ounces of corn sugar dissolved into 2 cups of water. Bottled on 5/26.

Got 25 twelve ounce bottles
