Notes

Date Note
02/06/2015 3:00am

Yeast starter! 80g of Pilsen Light DME and 800ml of water mixed in a 1L Erlenmeyer flask. Brought SLOWLY to a boil. Boiled 5 mins. Chilled down in ice bath. Pitched yeast at 60F. Put in lager fridge set at 50F on spin plate.

Researched pitch rates from Brewer's Friend later:
http://www.brewersfriend.com/yeast-pitch-rate-and-starter-calculator/
Calculator says I will end with 214 billion cells, recommends 365 billion.

02/07/2015 5:07pm

Boiled 1 gal water, added to mash tun. Heated 3.5 gal strike to 160+.
