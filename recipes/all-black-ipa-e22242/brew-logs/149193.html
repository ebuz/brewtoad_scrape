All Black IPA

a Partial Mash American IPA by Rollermt

Brewed by Rollermt

On April 30, 2017

Brewing Notes

*Made this beer with Patrick D.

Prep: - Preboiled top up water - Made yeast starter with 1 pint water + .5 cup DME. Pitched yeast when wort was a little warmer than usual. But starter was foaming and seemed normal. - Store didn't have Nelson Sauvin or other NZ hops other than Motueka. Need to plan a variant or another brew to these hops.

"Mash:" - Steeped 1# carafa grains in clean water, in fridge. Goal is for mostly color, no flavor. (~36 hours) - Steeped .5# of Crystal in 8 cups of 160 degree water. 30 min. No additional heat added per BCS book instructions. Book called for 1 gallon but I reduce this to half gallon.

Boil - SG was 1.050 so I boiled another 1# of pils DME and added to wort. Could have been because we didn't add enough honey (or it didn't have as much fermentables as estimated), because we didn't mash the crystal/carafa, because we didn't scrape the LME bucket very well, because our boil wasn't very strong, or a combo of all of the above added up to about 1 gravity point. - OG came up to 1.056 - I also don't think those hops are 16% alpha. Sample wasn't too bitter or aromatic, but maybe that's just our recipe since it has a lot of late hops.

Chill - 20 minutes, seemed fairly typical process. - Added honey after chill (because we forgot it earlier) - Poured between 2 buckets to aerate - 4x.

OG: 1.056

Ferment: - Pitched at 75 degrees (it was the end of the night, otherwise I would have chilled it more) - Put in cool with water and ice. Hoping the temp drops a little. - After a day, temp was at 63-64, then started to see some activity. Removed from swamp chiller water, ice. - Continued to ferment around 64 for 5 days. - Had to change to a blow off tube, krausen was pretty active and the bucket fairly full. Made a mess. - On Day 6 (Saturday), I changed the lid and airlock out for a clean one and brought up to the first floor. Krausen still high. Hoping to warm up at ~68-72 for finishing. - On Day 15 (Monday), krausen was still on top but started to fall. I think this beer was done faster but the krausen didn't drop. - SG: 1.010 - Dry hopped with 2 oz. of Galaxy (no bag). Started on 5/15 (14 days) - Primed with 4.1 oz. honey - FG: 1.012 (5.78% abv) - must have misread earlier reading.
