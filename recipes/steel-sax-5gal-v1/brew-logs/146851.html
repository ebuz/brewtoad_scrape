Steel Sax 5gal v1

an All Grain American Pale Ale by Stuart

Brewed by Stuart

On December 07, 2016

Brewing Notes

Mash Schedule

Strike grains with 4.98 gal of water at 159.2 °F. Mash at 150 °F for 60 min. Vorlauf and lauter 3.36 gal in your first runnings. Add 3.02 gal of sparge water at 168 °F. Vorlauf and lauter 3.02 gal in your second runnings. Your combined runnings should be 6.38 gal.

Water Additions

3.5 g Gypsum 8 g Calcium Chloride .9 tsp Lactic Acid to mash .4 tsp Lactic Acid to Sparge

Starter

2.4 l Water 8.52 oz DME Harvested 1318 with a fresh packet for ~140 bil starting cells - Blend with Scottish 1728 or London Ale 1028 or Thames Valley II 1882 to make 1.037 starter grow to 411 bil cells harvest 1 l for ~170 bil cells pitch 240 bil cells @ 60 F
