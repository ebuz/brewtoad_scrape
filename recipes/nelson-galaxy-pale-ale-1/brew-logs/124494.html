Taste

The flavor isn't quite as intense as the first time, but not bad. There is a sharpness to it that isn't the best, but not horrible. I am not sure if it is a mineral thing or a ferment thing that I am tasting. The body is a bit thin so I added some maltodextrin (3.5oz). I feel that it is probably a fermentation thing, so I might work with that, maybe a different yeast.

Notes

Date Note
08/31/2014 5:59pm

Added centennial at 30 minutes instead of 40 to turn bitterness down a bit.
Added whirlfloc and 1.25g of yeast nutrient at 25 minutes.
Left whirlpool hops in for 20 minutes stirring 3 times and then chilled for 15 minutes. Down to 90F. Did whirlpool and let it sit for 30 minutes before putting into carboy.

Ended up with 5 gallons in the fermenter, so needed to add 0.5 gallons of top up water. Original gravity was high (1.050) so watering it down that much will result in 1.045.

09/02/2014 12:53am

Pitched rehydrated pack of US-05 and a tube of Clarity Ferm (gluten treatment). No visible activity after 12hr, but that isn't too surprising at 64F.
09/23/2014 2:34am

Added some maltodextrin dissolved in a bit of water since the body was a bit thin.
