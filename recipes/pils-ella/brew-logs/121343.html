07/06/2014 11:53pm

9:53am: Mashed in with 9L water at 58.5°C and hit 51.0°C mash temp.
10mins: Added 5L water at 74°C and hit 59.0°C mash temp.
20mins: took 4L thick decoction, raised to 72°C for conversion (10mins)
30mins: boiled decoction for 10mins.
46mins: returned 4L decoction and hit 64.0°C mash temp; gas on LOW to maintain. Mash pH 5.44 from sample taken 10minutes later.
66mins: mash at 65.5°C; kept gas on LOW.
91mins: took 4L thin decoction and brought to boil.
112min: returned decoction to main mash and hit 72.0°C mash temp. Mash pH 5.49.
122min: finished vorlauf and began sparge.
166min: finished sparge. Collected 27L in 44mins (0.61L/min). Wort had SG of 1.044 (90% efficiency).
180min: boil started.
255min: finished boil, started chill.
261min: chilled in 6mins. 22L into fermenter, but I'll lose another 1.0-1.5L to cold break after racking again.
