Notes

Date Note
09/12/2013 3:41am

Boiled 80g of Breiss Light DME with 800mL of refrigerator filtered water for 15 minutes. Cooled to 72F. Measured 8.6% brix / 1.034 SG. Pitched 1 cial WLP007 Dry English Ale yeast.

Roasted 4oz raw walnuts at 250F - 350F for 20 mins. Pulverized in food processor. Poured 1.5 cups of Smirnoff Triple Distilled vodka over the ground nuts and began a steep.
09/14/2013 2:50pm

Boiled 1 gal to heat tun. Added. Heated 6.5 gal strike to 165F. Lost roughly 0.25 strike to spillage. Mashed-in at 152F. Decanted top layer of yeast starter after 11 hours in the refrigerator (measured 1.002 SG). Added ph Stabilizer 5^2 (1 tbsp.). Heated 3 gal sparge to 182F. Added until water reached top of the grain bed in the tun. 163.5F. Mashed 60 mins with stirring at 15 min intervals. Recycled & Drained. Added enough sparge water to cover grains. Rested 15 mins. Recycled & Drained. Added remainder of the sparge. Recycled and drained. Filled kettle. Had roughly 1/2 gallon left after third runnings.
09/14/2013 8:15pm

Boiled 71 minutes. Hop addition at 14 minutes (Centennial, Northdown and whirfloc). Mixed 2 cups of wort with 8 oz of Valrhona 100% cocoa powder and added homogenous liquid at 7 minutes. Flameout at 12:43 PM. Cooled to 85F (down to 90F in 30 mins). Aerated, pitched yeast and put in fermenter. Refractometer hard to read. Measured w/ hydrometer at 1.072 OG.
09/17/2013 5:33am

Measured gravities. Increased temp on ferm fridge from 65F to 68F.
11/06/2013 5:36am

Measured gravity at 9.8% brix. Bottled with 5 oz dextrose boiled for a few minutes in 2 cups of water. Added 1 oz harvested walnut liquor and 1 oz BrewCraft Hazlenut.
