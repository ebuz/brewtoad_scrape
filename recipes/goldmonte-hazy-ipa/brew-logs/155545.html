Goldmonte Hazy IPA

an All Grain American IPA by Matt Tremonte

  • Ingredients
  • 1 Brew Logs
  • 0 Variants
  • 0 Reviews
Print
Export BeerXML

Brewed by Matt Tremonte

On September 30, 2018

Brewing Notes

Oh man this beer smelled SO GOOD going into the fermenter.

Did a yeast starter on Friday night (~36 hour before fermentation) with the defined yeast and 3.5 oz of DME boiled in 1L of water with 1/2 tsp of Yeast Nutrien

Fermented in big mouth bubbler.
